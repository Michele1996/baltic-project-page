BALTIC

A Benchmark and Cross-Domain Strategy for 3D Reconstruction Across Air and Underwater Domains Under Varying Illumination

📄 Paper 💻 Code 📦 Full Dataset ⚡ Mini Dataset

Cross-domain reconstruction: underwater + in-air views improve robustness.

Overview

BALTIC is a controlled benchmark for evaluating 3D reconstruction methods across air and underwater environments under varying illumination. It enables systematic analysis of scattering, absorption, and domain shifts.

The devkit includes loaders, calibration parsing, and scripts to reproduce benchmark results from the paper.

Benchmark Protocol

  • Tasks: 3D reconstruction, cross-domain generalization
  • Metrics: PSNR, SSIM, LPIPS, Chamfer Distance
  • Evaluation: train/test splits across domains
